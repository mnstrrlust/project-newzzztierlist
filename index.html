Criteria
  • This tier list will follow essentially the same criteria as prydwen.gg, but with some key differences.
  • For DPS/Sub DPS agents, I will be strictly comparing agents of the same specialty with each other. For example, Attack agents will only be compared to other Attack agents, and not Anomaly or Rupture agents.

    However, as current endgame content isn't favoring Anomaly as much, Anomaly Primary DPS agents will be rated lower than Attack/Rupture characters depending strictly on how endgame treats them.
